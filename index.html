DEGANVY, U.K

Deganwy is a small town in Conwy County, Wales, situated along the scenic North Wales coast. It has a population of around 3,900 and is historically known for Deganwy Castle, which dates back to the 6th century as the stronghold of King Maelgwn Gwynedd.
